Why coffee is so popular?

The top reasons why coffee become trend culture are more than simply the caffeine content. A cup of coffee is the first thing millions of people reach for every morning and there are a multitude of reasons. First, the caffeine, plays there major role, which helps to get people energized. This is one of the top reasons that many people drink coffee.
Coffee has also become a very social drink similar in popularity to alcohol. Mornings at the local coffee shop are the place to hang out with friends or meet to discuss business. Coffee drinkers say they drink coffee to relax.
Finally, there are millions of people who simply love the taste of coffee. This taste is different for every coffee drinker because of the vast variety of coffee flavours, roasts and varieties available on the market.
So why not to try our coffee?
But first let’s find out what is difference between the two most known varieties of coffee plant.

ARABICA


-has a very wide taste range. The range differs from sweet-soft to sharp-tangy.When unroasted,beans smell like blueberries. Their roasted smell is described as perfumey with notes of fruit and sugar tones.

-are fragile and must grow in cool, subtropical climates. Arabica beans also need a lot of moisture, rich soil, shade, and sun.

-contains the 1.5 percent caffeine

ROBUSTA


-has a neutral to harsh taste range and is often likened to having an “oatmeal-like” taste. When unroasted, the smell of Robusta beans is described as raw-peanutty.


-come from a resilient plant that is able to be grown in low altitudes of 200-800 meters.

-contains coffeine at 2.7 percent
